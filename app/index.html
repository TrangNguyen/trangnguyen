I’m a self-taught programmer

I’m an autodidact developer and a cross-disciplinarian, having graduated in film editing, I make films, especially photofilms and installations. While looking for a technological solution to my video installation work, I was introduced to NodeJS and I was thrilled. Later with MongoDB and AngularJS I made myself a full-stack developer.

I’m a doer

Whatever I set myself into, I’ve excelled at it, whether it’s making exquisite meals or knitting a motherboard pattern. I take great pride in translating challenging design concepts into shipping applications and find an incredible reward seeing the result of my hours of hard work online.

I’m a teamplayer

As a filmmaker, I’ve mastered teamwork. There isn’t a product that is just for me to keep on my shelf but to share, to communicate. I respect my client’s interest, appreciate a good design, put myself in the shoes of the ones who will be using the product and combine all input, critic for the best of the project.

I’m a perfectionist

I love planning, I do extensive research, prototype, test and iterate, that goes for coding as much as for filmmaking. A project is not finished until I have seen the result, had a wrap-up with involved parties and drawn lessons for the next.

And what more, I love challenge. So if you have one, let’s talk about it.
